Dark Mode

Functionality

Our website now supports Dark Mode based on your operating system's preferences! If your operating system is set to Dark Mode, our site will automatically adapt to match your preference. This ensures a seamless and comfortable browsing experience, whether you're working during the day or night.

This feature is compatible with all browsers using the latest version.

Customize your experience

You also have the option to override your operating system preferences by selecting your preferred color scheme directly on our website. If you choose to do so, your preference will be stored in your browser's local storage as "preferred-dark-mode". This means your choice will persist and be remembered for future visits.
